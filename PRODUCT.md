# Product

## Register

product

## Users

OpenBBQ is for individual video workflow users who move videos from external platforms into a local transcription, translation, subtitle, and review pipeline. They are comfortable with automation and want the desktop app to make repeated media-language tasks faster, more visible, and less fragile without forcing them into CLI details.

## Product Purpose

OpenBBQ turns local files or remote video sources into inspectable subtitle workflows. Success means a user can import a source, understand the planned workflow, run or monitor automation, review generated transcript and translation segments, and export useful results with confidence in what happened at each step.

## Brand Personality

Clear, practical, and calm. The interface should feel like a readable workspace for focused media operations: approachable enough for one-person workflows, but precise enough for users who care about automation state, artifacts, and repeatability.

## Anti-references

Avoid flashy gradients, noisy decorative effects, and Chinese internet tool-site styling. Do not make the desktop app feel like a marketing page, a dark terminal skin, or a pile of identical promotional cards. Avoid visual choices that make automation status, source handling, or review work harder to scan.

## Design Principles

- Make the workflow visible: users should always understand source, state, next action, and output.
- Keep automation approachable: expose powerful behavior through clear steps, stable labels, and predictable controls.
- Prefer calm density: support repeated work with compact information hierarchy, not sparse promotional layouts.
- Preserve trust: state changes, errors, retries, and generated artifacts should be explicit and easy to verify.
- Stay local-first: the desktop surface should feel like a focused workspace, not a web campaign.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for core text and controls. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, reduced-motion preferences, and status indicators that do not rely on color alone.
